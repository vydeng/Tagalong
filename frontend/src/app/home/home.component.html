About Us

This webapp is a social networking site that aims to connect students based on mutual needs, which are broadly categorized as academic, errands, and events. For example, users can create an academic post looking for a study buddy, or an events post looking for a ride to a concert and is willing to bring car snacks. We encourage users to collaborate and build connections in a way that is more sustainable, budget-friendly, and community focused.
